EX-01 — EXHIBIT NOTE

Rebuilding Onboarding

RoleLead PM
TimelineQ2 2023 — 14 wks
Team2 eng, 1 design, 1 research

Problem

New users were dropping off before reaching their first meaningful action, and the onboarding flow had been built ad hoc, one feature at a time, with no unified narrative.

Approach

Ran 12 user interviews to map the gap between signup intent and first value. Reframed onboarding as a guided setup rather than a feature tour, prioritizing one clear "aha" moment.

Outcome

Activation improved by 40% within two release cycles. This is placeholder copy — each of the ten exhibits would get its own full case study here.
